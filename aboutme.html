About Me

Hello, I'm Camille Girodengo, a French student in the last year of her Bachelor's Degree in both Computer Science and Video Games Programming who aims to one day become a gameplay programmer.
I'm currently an exchange student between IUT Aix-Marseille in France and UQAC in Québec for a double degree following which I will obtain 2 Third Year University degrees : one in Computer Science (France) and the other in video games development (Canada).
I am most at ease with the programming languages C# and C++ that I often use with the Unity and Unreal game engines, though I also know Java, Php and JavaScript for app & website development.

My experiences

I don't quite yet have a lot of experience in the computer science or video game industries currently but I have done projects during my time in university such as a social-media like website in Php, an educational serious game about Earth's cycles for children in school and others. Such works helped me gain practical knowledge and organisational skill in applying agile management.
My university projects are not my only source of experience in the domain. I also did an internship in the virtual reality research center CVRM (Centre de Réalité Virtuelle de la Méditerranée) where I coded a plugin to connect and interact with BLE devices. Moreover, I had the occasion to develop a VR escape game called Labscape with fellow interns from different domains (2D & 3D Art, Game Design) to demonstrate the possibilities of virtual reality and hand tracking using the Unity game engine.
